Stand By Me Review


Robert Cady

October 23, 2025

Stand By Me is a coming-of-age film that follows 4 friends as they embark on a journey to find the body of a missing classmate who was hit by a train. The movie follows them as they hike and camp through the Orgeon forest, all while trying to avoid the local gang that want to find the body for money. This is a very nostalgic movie as it depicts the struggles of growing up and maturing. This is a must see movie for anyone who enjoys a simple plot with a deep underlying message about life and friendship.

T R I V I A

  • Rob Reiner considers this the best film he has ever made
  • Wil Wheaton's grandfather starred in Wagon Train (1957) so his grandmother asked that "Wagon Train" somehow be worked into the dialogue, and it was
  • Michael Jackson was asked to do a cover of the song, Stand By Me by Ben E. King, however they ultimately ended up using King's version
